Family seems dramatic, society feels annoying and the world appears broken, not because of the external chaos but because of the internal one. If we have chaos inside, it naturally expands into what we experience in the world. Most of us live throughout our life blaming him, her, this and that for the chaos, without realizing it was there right at our cores.

The same is similar for peace. If there is an inner peace, we feel the world as worth living, thoughts are calmer, relationships are balanced, and positivity all around. Just as chaos expands outward, the peace does the same.

So, the one solution to any problem is to remove the inner chaos but the question is how do we start?
